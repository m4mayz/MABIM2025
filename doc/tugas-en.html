C. Daily Assignments & Attendance

For General Participants

  1. Participants must fill out the online and offline attendance (gform) provided by the committee.
  2. Participants must complete the daily assignments.
  3. Online attendance is a personal photo during the event (documentation/photo can be taken before the event starts or before leaving).
  4. The daily assignment is to make a resume of the material each day. All material resumes must be made in 1 PDF document. The resume must be handwritten and include Full Name and Group number.
  5. Personal photo attendance and material resume photos are uploaded to the gform link provided by the committee.
  6. The deadline for attendance and daily assignments is at 11:00 PM WIB. After that time, the link will no longer be accessible.

For Employee Participants

  1. Participants must follow the entire MABIM series from beginning to end, by always filling out the attendance provided by the committee.
  2. Must fill out the specified attendance link.
  3. The attendance link will be closed by the committee at 11:00 PM WIB.
  4. Must create a resume for each designated material.
  5. Must include a photo of the participant with the MABIM YouTube live stream screen as proof of attendance (photo in neat attire).
  6. Must obey all MABIM rules and regulations.
  7. Must watch the live stream on the @MabimUnsp YouTube account.
  8. Must attend the Peak MABIM event. If unable to attend, provide a letter and reason from the relevant institution/company. Failure to attend the entire MABIM event will result in disqualification and no certificate, unless with a valid reason.
