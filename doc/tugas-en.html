CONSEQUENCES

  1. Failure to complete the Twibbon and video will reduce your passing grade.
  2. Absences due to illness/excuse are mandatory, requiring you to create a video presentation of the MABIM material of at least 3 minutes, upload it to your Instagram account, and complete the daily assignments and submit them to your mentors.
  3. Failure to complete the daily assignments requires you to create a poster, upload it to your Instagram feed, and submit it to your mentors.
  4. Failure to follow student organization and campus accounts will reduce your passing grade.
  5. If the coupon/stamp does not meet the requirements (minimum 4 UKM/UKMK and 1 study program for each participant or other study program), you must create a 100-word handwritten resume of your HMJ/UKMK/UKM, in accordance with the missing HMJ/UKMK/UKM stamp.

SUBMISSION

  1. Twibon must be uploaded to Instagram feed

  3. Photos of vouchers/stamps must be attached to Google Drive
  4. Screenshot evidence must be attached to Google Drive
